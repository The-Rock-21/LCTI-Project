What is the gift?

Dress-up costumes are themed outfits for children to pretend to be princesses, superheroes, or professionals like doctors and astronauts.

How does the gift work?

Costumes often include clothing, accessories, and props that transform the wearer into a character. Girls can put them on easily and immerse themselves in imaginative role-play.

How does the gift interest girls?

Dress-up costumes allow girls to express creativity, confidence, and aspirations through play. They enjoy embodying characters or roles that inspire them.

What is the price?

Costumes typically cost between $20 and $50, depending on the quality and accessories.

Where can I buy the gift?

You can buy them in most stores, or you can buy them online!

Summary

Dress-up costumes are fun, empowering gifts that inspire creative role-play. They are ideal for fostering confidence and imagination in young girls.
